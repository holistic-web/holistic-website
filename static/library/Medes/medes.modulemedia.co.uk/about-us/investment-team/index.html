The Team

Clemente Cappello

Founder and CEO

Clemente is Founder and CEO of the Investment Manager.

He has an 11 year continuous track record of investing in Central Asia and one year of investing in Iran. Clemente holds a degree in Business Administration and Finance from Bocconi University. Before founding Sturgeon he worked as an analyst for Haussmann Holdings. Clemente was awarded "Rising Star of Hedge Funds" in 2008 by Institutional Investor and has been quoted by the Financial Times, New York Times, Bloomberg, Fox News and other media outlets as an expert on Iranian and Central Asian markets.

Kiyan Zandiyeh

Portfolio Manager

Kiyan is a portfolio manager focusing on managing the firm's Iranian fund – Sturgeon Central Asia Fund – Class C Iran.

Prior to joining the Investment Manager, Kiyan covered emerging market equities at Roubini Global Economics, focusing on advising on equity strategy in the region. He previously worked at the London Stock Exchange. He is a British Iranian dual national and travels to Iran at least once a month, where he maintains an extensive network of contacts at companies listed on the Tehran Stock Exchange across a range of sectors. He is a graduate of Imperial College Business School.

Lanna Gregory

Operations and Admin

Lanna joined Sturgeon Capital in February 2013 assisting with operations and admin.

She previously worked for the Financial Services Compensation Scheme as part of the Investment Claims Team. Lanna has worked for the International Finance Corporation (IFC), part of the World Bank Group, where she was responsible for operations reports and general administration. Lanna holds an MBA from Imperial College, London with a concentration in Investment Management. She is also working towards full completion of the Investment Management Certificate.

Lawrence Head

Capital Raising and Investor Relations

Lawrence joined the Sturgeon Capital team in July 2016 from a background in Private Banking.

Initially he worked for Coutts & Co in their international private banking division, on the Middle East team. He later joined Plurimi Wealth LLP, a wealth management boutique specialising in bespoke investment solutions for Middle Eastern and Russian private clients. Lawrence is responsible for the firm’s Capital Raising & Investor Relations. 

Taco Sieburgh Sjoerdsma, CFA

CFO

Taco is CFO at Sturgeon Capital focusing on operations and research.

He has over 20 years of experience in the alternative investment industry. Specifically he was previously Head of Research for UFG focused on Russia, as well as Head of Research in Singapore for Paribas. He has worked as a senior analyst for both a global macro hedge fund and for an emerging markets treasury fund. He was also a rated analyst for UBS. In addition to being a CFA charter holder, Taco also holds an MBA from the University of Chicago.

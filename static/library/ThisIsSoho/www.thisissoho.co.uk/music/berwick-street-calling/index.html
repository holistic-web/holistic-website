Berwick Street Calling: Vote Now!

Berwick Street called out to the nation in search of the next unsigned artist. Now it’s over to you.

<iframe width="100%" height="166" scrolling="no" frameborder="no" src="https://w.soundcloud.com/player/?url=https%3A//api.soundcloud.com/tracks/267948756&amp;color=ff9900&amp;auto_play=false&amp;hide_related=false&amp;show_comments=true&amp;show_user=true&amp;show_reposts=false"></iframe>

You can give one lucky winner the chance to record with renowned producer Danton Supple in a local Soho studio and win their single pressed onto 1,000 7" vinyl. The winning single will be available in selected Berwick Street stores this Autumn.

Plus, the winner will now also get the incredible opportunity of playing at OnBlackheath Festival in partnership with John Lewis. Taking place 10-11 September, OnBlackheath is the ultimate weekend for standout live music with mouth-watering food and drink experiences. Headliners include Primal Scream, Grammy Award winning electronic music maestros Hot Chip, alongside Belle & Sebastian, James, alongside live stages from the likes of NTS and Heavenly Recordings. 
 
The shortlist has been selected by a panel of leading music experts including NME’s music editor Matt Wilkinson, Primal Scream's bassist Simone Butler, Music Director of The House of St Barnabas Sav Remzi and owner of Sister Ray, Phil Barton.
 
Listen below and vote for your favourite artist. Click on their name to learn more about them and the song. Voting closes 23 June.

Check out our SoundCloud for the full playlist.
 
Follow @BerwickStLondon to find out who will win #BerwickStreetCalling.

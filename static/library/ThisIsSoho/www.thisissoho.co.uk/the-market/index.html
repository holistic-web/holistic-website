The Market

Berwick Street Market is one of London's oldest markets dating back to 1778.

It's now a bustling foodie destination full of concept traders include Savage Salads and Jerusalem Falafel, all sitting alongside traditional fruit and vegetable and florist stalls.
Find the market open Monday to Saturday from 8am until 6pm and at its busiest at lunchtimes.

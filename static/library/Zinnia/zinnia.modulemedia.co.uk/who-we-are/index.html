HOW WE DID IT

Established in 1999, we are a team of experienced business people , who have come through the ranks to perform at a senior level in a variety of industries, including: manufacturing, retail, hotel and catering, arts, media, public sector, finance, education and IT.

We train and coach to the highest industry standards, and work to a strict code of conduct, which guarantees honesty and confidentiality - both at company and individual level - at all times. Doing an outstanding job is paramount at Zinnia, and for that reason we will only take on an assignment where we can be sure we will deliver. Testament to this, is the fact that 95% of our clients have come to us through personal recommendations and referrals.

Who We Are

OUR EXPERIENCE

We are all experienced business people who have worked our way through the ranks. We passionately believe in captivating your people and listening to their ideas on what will make your business great. We help people to stand out and stand up for what they think they need to do or needs to be done and we equip people with the skills and confidence to do it.

We want people to instinctively understand how to develop great relationships based on trust and collaboration. We spend time with your team teaching them to have better conversations and really think about how they are moving their career forward. We invite people to constantly challenge themselves and grow professionally and personally.

OUR TEAM

About Pam Bateson

Pam Bateson is an expert coach, consultant and trainer with 22 years experience working at senior and board level in both the private and public sectors. She has held managerial positions across a wide variety of sectors including retail (J Sainsbury and Asda), hotels (Crest and Forte), NHS (Carlisle Hospitals Trust) and manufacturing (Pinney’s of Scotland, Unigate/Uniq plc).

Her key strength is helping people and teams to achieve personal growth and development, ensuring greater alignment to business objectives invariably adding value to their roles. Whilst at Pinney’s, she helped transform the business from loss to profit through the creation and implementation of a complex organisational redesign programme. At Carlisle Hospitals she was instrumental in implementing the change programme associated with 4th wave trust status in her role as head of training & organisational change. Pam has been running Zinnia since 1999, is a Fellow of the Chartered Institute for Personnel and Development, a trained coach and teacher.

About Frances Cairney

Frances Cairney is a skilled coach and marketing consultant with more than 20 years experience. A qualified M.A. working in both the public and private sectors, she applied her specialist knowledge in some of the country’s top arts and media organisations including Scottish Opera, smg, The Herald and the Edinburgh International Festival Fringe.

As a senior marketeer, she has been responsible for award-winning campaigns both in the UK and overseas and operated at board level in a number of organisations. She has been a guest lecturer at Stirling & Glasgow Universities and regularly presents at conferences. A fully trained coach, she brings her commercial and artistic experience to businesses to enable real and positive change to the working environment.

About Nick Skellon

Nick Skellon is a highly experienced professional with a passionate commitment to service excellence. Since leaving the Army as a Captain, he spent fifteen years in a variety of blue-chip, FMCG companies such as Mars Confectionery, Duracell, Rothmans (UK), Golden Wonder and Nichols Vimto in a variety of roles, from salesman to Sales and Marketing Director.

With his deep knowledge amassed over more than a decade of specialism in customer service, he has worked with companies both large and small to develop a sustainable competitive edge through the delivery of service excellence. He has succeeded in providing this in a variety of industries including Guinness UDV, Institute of Directors, Liverpool Football Club, Royal Sun Alliance, Siemens, British Airways, CNN Fox Network and Barclays Bank.

About Maria Chase

Using Neuroscience as an innovative catalyst for clearer thinking, Maria specialises in designing bespoke opportunities for companies and professionals to discover fresh perspectives and develop cutting edge solutions to today’s challenges. With 18 years of experience working across all strategic levels, both nationally and internationally, she shares simple and elegant strategies to decode the machinery of the mind and empower clients to get where they want to be both professionally and personally.

About Zoe Creighton

Zoe Creighton is an experienced coach, facilitator and trainer, working with a wide variety of individuals and teams to increase their effectiveness and achieve their challenging goals. Prior to this she spent 20+ years in marketing with Unilever, Kimberly-Clark and Diageo. She lead multi-national and multi-functional teams across Europe in strategic planning, NPD and advertising on brands such as Persil, Kleenex and Pimms. Her business experience ensures her approach to facilitation and people development is realistic and action oriented to deliver positive changes in behaviour and thinking, building resilience and confidence along the way.

She has developed coaching programmes for organisations as diverse as an international technology services provider, a major charity and a design agency. Her facilitation and training work has included supporting organisations to increase their diversity at very senior levels, for example delivering an Emerging Leaders Programme designed to develop leadership skills and personal impact.
